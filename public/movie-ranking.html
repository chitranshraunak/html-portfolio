The Best Movies According to Chitransh

My top 3 movies of all-time.

A Beautiful Mind

Nash's story is really full of love for excellence above anything and a fight against himself to keep moving on that path of mathematics. .

Ramanujan

Found it fascinating how a boy from Madras dives deep into the pool of mathematics and contributes to the field with many fresh and useful ideas solving problems.

Super-30

This movie is a testimonial to the fact that you don't need huge amount of money to make it to a higher institute of the highest value in a country.
All you need is a will to study,experiment,explore and stay curious all the while..
